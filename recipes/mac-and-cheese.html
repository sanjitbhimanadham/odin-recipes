Slow Cooker Mac and Cheese

Description

This crockpot mac and cheese is creamy, comforting, and takes just moments to assemble in a slow cooker. Great for large family gatherings and to take to potluck dinners. It's always a big hit!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese
  • Milk products
  • Eggs
  • Canned soup
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water, then drain and transfer to the slow cooker.
  2. Stir in the butter, seasonings, and about half of the Cheddar.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Whisk the evaporated milk and eggs together, then stir into the pasta.
  5. Whisk the milk and the soup together, then stir into the pasta.
  6. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over the pasta mixture and garnish with paprika.
  7. Cook on Low for 2 Â½ to 3 hours.
